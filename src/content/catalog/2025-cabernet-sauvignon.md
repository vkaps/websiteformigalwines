This batch originates from carefully selected estate parcels at 400–600m elevation in the Douro Valley, harvested at peak ripeness under strict phytosanitary protocols. Grapes were destemmed, crushed, and fermented in temperature-controlled stainless steel tanks (18–22°C) over a 14-day maceration period, followed by full malolactic conversion for textural roundness and enhanced stability.

Post-fermentation laboratory analysis confirms consistent batch parameters across all tanks. Full Certificate of Analysis (COA) including heavy metals, sulphites, and microbiological profiling available upon request to qualified trade buyers.

## Technical Notes

This lot is held in sealed, nitrogen-blanketed stainless steel tanks at 14°C pending dispatch. Stability testing has been conducted including cold stabilization and protein fining with bentonite. The wine is suitable for immediate bottling or further oak maturation by the buyer.
