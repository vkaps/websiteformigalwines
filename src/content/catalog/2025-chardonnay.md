This Chardonnay lot was harvested at 20 Brix to retain high natural acidity, characteristic of our early-harvest white wine program in the Alentejo. Cold maceration and direct press followed by fermentation at 14–16°C in stainless steel jacketed tanks preserves the varietal freshness without the influence of oak. The wine underwent partial malolactic conversion (30%) for balance and textural complexity.

Full analytical data pack available for qualified buyers, including sensory evaluation sheets from our internal QC laboratory. This lot carries Vegan Certified status (no animal-derived fining agents used).

## Technical Notes

The wine is held at 4°C post cold stabilization and sterile-filtered to 0.45 microns. Tank headspace is maintained under argon blanket. Buyer can specify residual SO2 levels within permitted ranges prior to dispatch.
